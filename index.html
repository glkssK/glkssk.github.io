ABOUT ME

Building motion, 3D, and vision-driven intelligence.

I am currently an undergraduate student at Changan University. From 2024 to 2026, I work with Prof. Xiaohua Xia on no-reference image blur assessment. Since 2025, I have also been fortunate to intern with Dr. Ronghui Li at Tsinghua University, where I focus on motion synthesis, digital humans, and generative AI for 3D.

My research interests lie in Motion Synthesis, Digital Humans, Generative AI for 3D, and Computer Vision. I am especially interested in connecting visual understanding, motion generation, and 3D content creation through learning-based methods.

Current Focus

Human motion & digital human generation

Exploring how generative models can bridge motion, interaction, and 3D character creation.

Vision Research

No-reference blur assessment

Working on stable and accurate blind focus assessment with blur-centric augmentation strategies.

Academic Goal

From perception to controllable generation

Interested in unifying visual perception, motion modeling, and generative learning in 3D spaces.
