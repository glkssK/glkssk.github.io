ABOUT ME

About Me

I am currently an undergraduate student at Changan University. From 2024 to 2026, I work with Prof. Xiaohua Xia at Changan University on no-reference image blur assessment. Since 2025, I have also been fortunate to intern with Dr. Ronghui Li at Tsinghua University, where I work on motion synthesis, digital humans, and generative AI for 3D.

My research interests include Motion Synthesis, Digital Humans, Generative AI for 3D, and Computer Vision. I am especially interested in connecting visual understanding, motion generation, and 3D content creation through learning-based methods.
